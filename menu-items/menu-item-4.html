Royal Coffee
Description
Inhale the delightful arabic coffee smell that fills Bahrain streets. Arabic coffee is considered a significant part of Bahrain culture and tradition. Cardamom is added to this coffee and poured from the arabic Dallah of golden or silver color, and served in small cups. Drinking arabic coffee with friends is an old social tradition and it is best usually with fresh dates
